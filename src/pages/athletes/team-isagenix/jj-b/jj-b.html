Former NFL Wide Receiver Scores Big with Isagenix

At 5’10" and 160 pounds, everyone said JJ B. was too small to play football.

But, after nine successful years as an NFL wide receiver for the Cleveland Browns (1988-1989), Dallas Cowboys (1989), Kansas City Chiefs (1990-1994) and the Atlanta Falcons (1994-1996), JJ proved his critics wrong. "I guess the important thing is I am an example of what can be achieved if you want it bad enough and you’re willing to put in the work," explains JJ.

Now he’s applying that same philosophy to a new venture.

Life After Football

After retiring from football in 1997, JJ did a brief stint in corporate America, but in 2007, he decided to embark on a career in network marketing with his wife, Raina.

For the next six years, they had great success, but were ready for change and looking for a new network marketing company to join forces with. That’s when they discovered Isagenix®.

"I actually made a list of eleven requirements that my next company had to meet," said JJ. "Isagenix met all of them, including my most important requirement—having premier products backed by science."

The business model also afforded JJ and his wife the time freedom they needed for their family.

Together, JJ and Raina have eight children: five nieces and nephews who they have adopted and three biological children of their own. "Our house is crazy," jokes this Scottsdale resident. "But we are all striving for the same goal."

The Ultimate Touchdown

With all of the presentations, videos, sales and marketing tools completely accessible through Isagenix, JJ knew he’d found a company that "got it right." "The compensation plan is great. People can make great money up front and residual commissions kick in quickly after building your team. It’s amazing!" says JJ. "Isagenix is the full package," he adds. "They have been doing it right from day one and continue to do so. I don’t believe there is a more solid network marketing opportunity out there today."
