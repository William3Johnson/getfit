Finding the Figures for Success

Lindsey had a desire to do fitness competitions for years but never thought she could. Then she had an epiphany at Isagenix® Celebration 2011. "I saw (Ms. Bikini Universe 2010, Ms. Bikini America 2010 and Ms. Figure America 2010) Lori Harder and all the Savage Girls and I realized I was using the same products they were. So why couldn’t I do it"?

With the help of Isagenix, the 29-year-old took second place at her first fitness competition last year. "People think fitness competitors are in the gym six hours a day but I was only in the gym an hour a day. It was incredible to see the changes to my body just from incorporating good nutrition." As she preps for her first national competition at Fitness America in November, Isagenix is helping Lindsey maintain her physique and natural energy. "Many people rebound after competing, but the amazing thing is I’ve stayed within a few pounds of my competition weight year-round and I feel great."

Fitness competitions have now become one of Lindsey’s biggest passions, along with helping others achieve ultimate health through her burgeoning Isagenix business, which she is now pursuing full time. "I am so happy to have found a business that combines my passion for personal development with health and wellness."
