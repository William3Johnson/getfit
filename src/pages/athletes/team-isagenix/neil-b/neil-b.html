Neil B. began his running career in high school. "I ran cross country in school and my coach saw potential in me. He suggested I start training with the senior squad and it went from there. Over the last two years I’ve started competing and taking my running much more seriously," shares Neil.

"I’d been having some problems with digestive discomfort during training, something commonly known as ‘runners gut;’ I’d been to see a number of nutritionists but nothing worked consistently. I approached a friend about how I could improve my nutrition and she introduced me to Isagenix.

"Initially, I thought it was a bit expensive but I took the plunge and started on the Athletes Pak. That has been the biggest improvement for me!"

Feeling less stressed during training and racing meant Neil started to see significant improvements in his racing times. "Since being on Isagenix I’ve recorded personal best times in 1500m, 5000m, 10000m, 10km, 12km and 14km which has been incredible. I’ve gone from 71kg to 66kg.** Isagenix is now a permanent fixture in my routine. I’m always looking for ways to improve and it’s very exciting that new products are coming out all the time."

Neil’s career highlight was coming 10th in the Sydney City to Surf in August 2012. "To place 10th in the world’s biggest road race was an amazing achievement for me. I’ve now moved to Sydney to work more closely with my coach and see what I can make of my running career.

"Many athletes have misconceptions about taking new products and supplements. Most of us are seeking out that extra couple of percent from our performance and look to our training to help with that. I never thought about nutrition as a potential performance gain but the nutritional power I have unlocked with Isagenix is incredible. It’s amazing what your body can achieve when you provide it with the right fuel."

Neil’s goals for the future are to continue improving on his personal best times and concentrate on staying injury free. He’s also building a business with Isagenix.
