No-Bake Almond Fudge

  • 1 large avocado
  • 1/4 almond milk or milk of choice
  • 2 scoops of IsaLean® Shake in Creamy Dutch Chocolate
  • 1 teaspoon of cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • ½ cup of crushed almonds
  • Natural sweetener to taste
  1. Add avocados, cocoa powder, IsaLean® Shake in Creamy Dutch Chocolate, sea salt and natural sweetener to a food processor. If you don’t have a food processor, add these ingredients into a large bowl and then beat with an egg beater. Process until smooth.
  2. Add the almonds into the smooth fudge batter. Beat again until the batter is smooth and/or chunky. Whichever you prefer.
  3. Line an 8 x 8 baking dish with parchment paper (not wax paper) and place the mixture from the food processor/bowl in it. Press firmly with the back of a spoon or spatula to remove any air.
  4. Freeze for at least three hours before serving. Remove the pan, slice into squares and sprinkle with coarse sea salt.
