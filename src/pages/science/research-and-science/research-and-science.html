Research and Science

The Isagenix Research and Science Team is responsible for spearheading clinical research on Isagenix products, reviewing evidence-based literature to substantiate product claims, and contributing innovative ideas for product development.

Comprised of nutritionists and registered dietitians, the R&S team heads up the discussions at Isagenix about health and nutrition. They are in constant communication with John Anderson, the Isagenix Scientific Advisory Board, and respected scientists who are experts in their fields to analyze the latest in scientific research. Through collaboration and review of scientific literature, the R&S team helps ensure that Isagenix products continue to undergo developments that are representative of the latest that science has to offer for living a life in optimal health.

The team also contributes to evidence-based nutrition research by partnering with several well-respected universities in funding and conducting clinical studies. These include University of Illinois at Chicago (UIC), Arizona State University, University of Maryland, Skidmore College, University of Colorado, and more. For example, UIC conducted a clinical study involving Isagenix products that was published in two peer-reviewed journals (Nutrition Journal and Nutrition & Metabolism). The study serves as clinical substantiation for Isagenix systems for healthy weight loss and also adds to advance our understanding of the relationship between diet, nutrition and health.

While keeping an ear to the ground for the latest developments in nutritional science, the R&S team also devotes time to communicating new scientific developments to Isagenix Associates through events, podcasts, and articles.
